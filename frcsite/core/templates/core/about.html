About Us

We are the Titanium Tigers, FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) Team 4829. We are a 30+ member team of Chapel Hill High School students who work together to build a competition robot in six weeks each year.

We are starting the 2018-19 season strong. We won THOR, the North Carolina FIRST Robotics pre-season tournament. The event drew 32 teams from across North Carolina, South Carolina, and Georgia. Along with teams from Apex and Cary, our 3-team alliance easily went on to win the best of 3 matches in the quarter, semis, and finals.
